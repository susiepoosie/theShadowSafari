How to walk in the dark

a small guide to tending your worries

  1. Carry the light. Move your hand and the matchstick follows — its little flame is the only light you have. Sweep it through the dark to uncover the words hidden there.
  2. Catch a worry. Lift your hands into the glowing Portal and hold them still. The light catches the shape they make and traps the worry as a small shadow creature.
  3. Watch them wander. Your creatures drift through the gloom and shy away from your flame. A curious few may come closer.
  4. Name it, then free it. Click a creature to give your worry a name. Naming a thing makes it smaller — and easier to let go.
  5. Let them gather. Trap as many as you like. When the dark grows crowded, your worries begin to consume one another, until only one remains.
  6. Find the light of your life. Shine a bright light at the screen — a phone flashlight will do — and the sun rises. Your worries melt into the warmth, and you begin again.

There is no score here. Only you, the dark, and a small flame to find your way.
